# Groundedness and Citation Evaluation

## Groundedness questions
- Is every material claim supported by retrieved context?
- Does the answer blend multiple sources correctly?
- Does the model state uncertainty when evidence is incomplete?

## Citation questions
- Is a citation present?
- Does the cited chunk actually support the claim?
- Is the cited source approved and current enough for the use case?

## Promotion note
Citation presence alone is not sufficient. Citation correctness must also be checked.
